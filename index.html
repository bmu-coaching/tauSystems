Our Mission

Solve complex challenges in hypercritical environments to advance scientific discovery and provide innovative solutions to better our community.

Intuitive

TAU-Systems brings years of experience designing instrumentation and control systems for a diversity of fluid environments. Specializing in additive manufacturing and design for novel instrumentation, TAU-Systems brings engineering theory to real world applications.

Powerful

Our ability to make a difference in the realm of engineering stems from a diverse team background capable of novel developments in the fields of hydrodynamics, robotics, instrumentation, renewable energy, and numerical modeling for a wide range of applications.

Secure

TAU-Systems' team is comprised of members holding several levels of DoD clearances and continually practices OPSEC for day-to-day operations ensuring end-user privacy.
